The AI Society @ UT Dallas Presents: Innovation Lab

A research-driven division where we focus on learning models and frameworks that enable us to release AI/ML projects that people will actually use.

Description

TaskMaster allows you to create achievable tasks from a course syllabus to prepare for your exam. Focusing on learning the user's behaviors to best customize the tasks. AskTemoc is a drop-in enhancement to the UT Dallas website that serves as a counselor and allows users to get speedy personalized answers based on their student details. ResearchMate aims to aid the paper-reading process by generating pre-reads, per-scroll live context, and a citations graph by using references and the contents of the papers.
